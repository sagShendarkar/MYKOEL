Kirloskar Vasundhara International Film Festival at Kolhapur
The Kolhapur chapter of the 'Kirloskar Vasundhara International Film Festival' (KVIFF) was held on September 18-24, 2024. The festival has been organized in the city as part of its India tour across 25 locations in six states.
